How to cancel your monthly subscription

Short intro
We want everything to be clear and respectful of your time.
If you’re currently subscribed to POP Plan or VIP Plan, you can cancel your monthly subscription yourself in a few steps. No phone calls.

Before you start (30 seconds)
You’ll need the email address you used when you subscribed.
If you don’t know your password (or never created one), you can use “Forgot password” on the login page to set one.
Step‑by‑step (most people finish in 2–3 minutes)

Step 1: Open the login page
Go to: https://systeme.io/login

Step 2: Log in (or set a password)
Enter your email address. It must be the same email address you used to pay for the subscription.
If you don’t remember your password, click “Forgot password” and follow the email instructions.

Step 3: Open your subscription settings
After logging in:
Click your profile picture (top right).
Click Settings.
In the left menu, click Manage my subscriptions.

Step 4: Cancel the right plan (POP or VIP)
Find the subscription you want to cancel (POP Plan or VIP Plan).
Click the three dots (⋯).
Click Cancel subscription.
Click Confirm.
That’s it.

What happens after you cancel? Your cancellation will take effect just before your next renewal date (so you won’t be charged again after that date).

Important note (to avoid confusion)
Unsubscribing from emails is not the same as canceling a paid subscription.
To stop the monthly payments, you must follow the steps above inside Manage my subscriptions.

If you can’t log in: First, try “Forgot password” on the login page https://systeme.io/login
If you still can’t access the account, contact us at sportofliving@gmail.com
